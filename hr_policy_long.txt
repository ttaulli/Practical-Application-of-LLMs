Acme Corporation Employee Handbook

1. Introduction
Welcome to Acme Corporation. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and productive environment where all employees can thrive.

2. Employment Policies
Acme Corporation is an at-will employer. This means employment can be terminated by either the employee or the company at any time, with or without cause and with or without notice.

3. Equal Employment Opportunity
We provide equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or genetics.

4. Workplace Conduct
All employees are expected to conduct themselves professionally. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated, including sexual harassment, bullying, or discrimination.

5. Attendance Policy
Regular attendance is essential. Employees should notify their supervisor as soon as possible if they are unable to report to work. Excessive absenteeism may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

6. Working Hours
Our standard business hours are Monday through Friday, 8:00 am to 5:00 pm. Employees are expected to be present during these hours unless alternative arrangements are approved.

7. Overtime
Non-exempt employees will be compensated for overtime in accordance with federal and state laws. All overtime must be pre-approved by a supervisor.

8. Employee Benefits
8.1 Health Insurance
Full-time employees are eligible for health, dental, and vision insurance starting on their first day of employment. Part-time employees working over 20 hours per week are eligible for health insurance after six months of continuous employment.

8.2 Vacation
Employees accrue vacation time based on years of service:
- First year: 10 vacation days
- Years 2-4: 12 vacation days
- Year 5 and beyond: 15 vacation days

8.3 Sick Leave
Employees receive 5 sick days per year. Sick leave may be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member.

8.4 Paid Holidays
Acme observes these paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

8.5 Retirement Plan
Acme offers a 401(k) retirement plan with company match up to 4% of salary for eligible employees after 90 days of employment.

8.6 Life Insurance
Basic life insurance is provided at no cost to full-time employees. Supplemental life insurance is available at group rates.

8.7 Employee Assistance Program
We offer an Employee Assistance Program (EAP) to help employees with personal or work-related issues.

9. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted annually to provide feedback, set goals, and discuss career development opportunities.

10. Professional Development
Acme encourages employees to pursue professional development and may reimburse costs for courses, certifications, or conferences related to their role, with prior approval.

11. Code of Ethics
Employees are expected to adhere to our Code of Ethics, which includes honesty, integrity, respect, and compliance with laws and regulations.

12. Confidentiality
Employees must maintain the confidentiality of company information, trade secrets, and customer data during and after employment.

13. Safety Policy
We are committed to maintaining a safe work environment. Employees should report any unsafe conditions or incidents to HR or their supervisor immediately.

14. Drug-Free Workplace
Acme maintains a drug-free workplace. The use, possession, or distribution of illegal drugs is strictly prohibited.

15. Disciplinary Actions
Violation of company policies may result in disciplinary action, including verbal warnings, written warnings, suspension, or termination.

16. Termination of Employment
Upon termination, employees must return all company property. Final paychecks will include payment for accrued but unused vacation time, as required by law.

17. Contact Information
For questions about these policies, contact Human Resources at hr@acmecorp.com.

End of Document
